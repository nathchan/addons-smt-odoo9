Customer Order Delivery Date

Know when your customers want products delivered


View Delivery Date on Checkout Page (Validate Order)

The Order Delivery Date app gives your customer the option to choose the date they want the delivery of the purchased products from your store. Customers can even leave comment while placing order. You can view it on checkout (Validate order) page.



Calendar Section

Customer can select delivery date from calendar. Previous date cannot be select as delivery date.



View Delivery Date with Comments

Customer Order Delivery Date with comments will get displayed in "Customer Order Delivery Date" tab in Sales Order Form View. If user does not insert comments, no comments will get displayed.



Disable this App from Back End

You can disable "delivery date" or "delivery comment" separately from website setting tab.
